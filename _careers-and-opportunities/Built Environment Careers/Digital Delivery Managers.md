<h4><strong>Digital Delivery Manager</strong></h4>
<p>Shape how Singapore builds smarter.</p>
<p>Before a single brick is laid, projects are built digitally.</p>
<p>Digital Delivery Managers make this possible using data, 3D models and
smart tools to plan, test and optimise projects before construction even
begins.</p>

<h4>&nbsp;<strong>What Does a Digital Delivery Manager Do?</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Develop and manage 3D digital models (BIM) across the project lifecycle</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Use data to predict issues and test solutions before construction</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enable collaboration through shared digital platforms (CDE)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Apply tools like VR, simulations and drones on-site</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Support teams in adopting digital workflows and technologies</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>You don’t just build projects.</p>
<p>You build them smarter before they even exist.</p>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
<h4><strong>Why This Role Matters</strong></h4>
<p>Singapore is transforming how it builds – becoming more digital, productive
and efficient.</p>
<p>Digital Delivery Managers help reduce errors, improve coordination and
ensure projects are delivered faster and more effectively.</p>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
<h4><strong>Did You Know?</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Projects are now built digitally first using 3D models before physical
construction begins.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Tools like VR and simulations allow teams to test ideas and detect issues
early.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Digital platforms connect all stakeholders across the project lifecycle
in real time.</p>
</li>
</ul>
