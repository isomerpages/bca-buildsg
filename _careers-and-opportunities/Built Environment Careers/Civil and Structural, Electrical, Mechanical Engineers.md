<p><strong>Engineers</strong>
</p>
<p>Engineer the systems that keep Singapore moving.</p>
<p>Every day, millions of people rely on infrastructure they rarely think
about – MRT lines, water systems, buildings and energy networks.</p>
<p>Engineers design, build and maintain these essential systems, creating
solutions that keep Singapore connected, resilient and ready for the future.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ToZTqfqUUDw" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Watch three young engineers share how they are shaping Singapore’s future – from building MRT lines and smart water systems to developing sustainable cooling solutions.</a>
</p>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>What Does an Engineer Do?</strong>
</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Design engineering solutions for
buildings and infrastructure</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Tackle complex technical challenges
using data and technology</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Work with architects, project managers
and construction teams</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Deliver safe, reliable and sustainable
infrastructure</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Transform ideas into infrastructure
that serves millions of people every day</p>
<p>Engineering is where innovation meets real-world impact.</p>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Why This Role Matters</strong>
</p>
<p>Singapore is continually investing in infrastructure to support future
generations.</p>
<p>Engineers make this possible by designing smarter, more resilient and
sustainable systems that improve everyday life and strengthen the nation’s
long-term development.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Did You Know?</strong>
</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Engineers are behind many of Singapore’s
landmark infrastructure projects, from MRT networks to smart water and
energy systems.</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Digital tools and simulations allow
engineers to test and optimise designs before construction begins.</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Engineering careers combine creativity,
technology and problem-solving to tackle real-world challenges.</p>
