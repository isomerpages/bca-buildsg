<p><strong>Experience Singapore's Built Environment</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
<p>Ever wonder what it takes to build a city like Singapore – from underground
MRT stations to sky-high buildings?</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Come discover at the Building Singapore Festival! Best of all, entry is
free for everyone!</p>
<p>
<br>Through interactive exhibits and activities, learn how our urban landscape
is thoughtfully designed, constructed, and maintained. Bring your whole
family for an exciting day of discovery and hands-on fun! Fun for the whole
family! Journey through 5 interactive zones with hands-on activities, interactive
exhibits, and digital games that take you behind the scenes of how our
city is planned, built, and maintained.</p>
