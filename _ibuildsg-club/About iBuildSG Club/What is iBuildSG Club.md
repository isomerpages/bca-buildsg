<p></p>
<p>Part of a broader initiative by the Building and Construction Authority
(BCA), the iBuildSG Club is designed for students — from secondary schools
to junior colleges, ITEs, polytechnics and universities — who are curious
about the Built Environment (BE) sector. The Club offers exclusive opportunities
to explore, learn, and grow through hands-on experiences.</p>
<hr>
<p></p>
<p>Are you a student curious about the Built Environment (BE) and the exciting
careers it offers?</p>
<p>The iBuildSG Club is for you! This BE-themed interest club is tailored
for students from secondary schools, junior colleges, ITEs, polytechnics
and universities.</p>
<p>As a member, you will receive updates on the Club's activities and exclusive
competitions just for members. Best of all, it’s free! <a href="https://form.gov.sg/5f113808dba1d90011ca8ff3" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Join the iBuildSG Club</a> today!</p>
