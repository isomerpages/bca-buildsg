<p>Kickstart your career in the Built Environment (BE) with this scholarship
opportunity!</p>
<p>Offered under the Singapore-Industry Scholarship (SgIS) programme, this
undergraduate scholarship is available to Singapore Citizens aspiring to
contribute to leading organisations in Singapore's BE sector. Explore the
list of participating <a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/sgis/sponsoring-organisations/industries/built-environment" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">BE sponsoring organisations</a>.</p>
<p>Applications open from January to March annually. <a href="https://brightsparks.com.sg/profile/buildsg/scholarship.php?schid=10410" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Apply here</a>!</p>
